The Vision

Away from the spotlight, our pets see the version of us that the world rarely witnesses. "Me and My Pet" is an unscripted journey into the homes of high achievers, exploring how empathy, responsibility, and love shape their extraordinary lives.

Authenticity First

No scripts. No "Take 2s." We follow a natural rhythm. Our host joins you in your sanctuary—whether it’s your garden, your living room, or your stable—for a genuine "day-in-the-life" experience.

World-Class Quality

As a DGO Original, we utilize a multi-camera 4K setup and high-fidelity audio to ensure your story is told with the cinematic prestige it deserves.
